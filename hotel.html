THE HOTEL

The room includes a double bed from Royal Eden and Geismar linen, bathroom with a shower, a mini bar stocked with nothing but the best, a flat screen television that can be hidden from view, a stereo where you can listen to the music of your choice and wifi.

Your stay includes coffee and tea during the opening hours at Central Café + a delicious breakfast served downstairs in the little café.

You are also invited to enjoy a drink on the house at our restaurant Granola just around the corner.

In additional services you can arrange to have a bottle of chilled champagne and chocolate ready in your room upon arrival.

The room is not suitable for families since the limited space simply doesn’t allow for extra beds.

The price is 2.500 DKK per night including all of the above mentioned.


Check in is at 13:00 p.m.
Check out is at 11:00 a.m.
