THE CAFÈ

This tiny café is a neighbourhood favourite.
Here the locals get their morning coffee to stay or to go and meet up with friends and neighbours. The place is cosy and lively at the same time.

CENTRAL HOTEL & CAFÈ is squeezed in between appartment buildings and is a staple for both the Frederiksberg- and Vesterbro area.
Come and join our welcoming staff who loves to have a chat whether you are a local regular or just visiting Copenhagen.
