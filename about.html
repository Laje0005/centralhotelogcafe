LOCATED IN THE VESTERBRO NEIGHBOURHOOD

Central Hotel og Café is located in the Vesterbro neighbourhood right on the border of Frederiksberg.

It is a lively and trendy area in secure distance to the city’s tourist traps. Around the corner is Værnedamsvej, the cosiest street in the city. Værnedamsvej is a small, busy shopping street situated between Vesterbro and Frederiksberg.

Here you will also find our beautiful restaurant Granola on Værnedamsvej 5. The street is filled with charm and a relaxed atmosphere where locals meet and hangout.

For nightlife there are many restaurants, bars and cocktail bars in the immediate area. Close by is the old meat packing district (Kødbyen), with clubs, galleries, cafés, bars and restaurants - not to be missed!

FROM A SHOEMAKER’S SHOP TO A TINY HOTEL

The story behind the one small room at Central Hotel and Coffee is both fascinating and unique. Originally, the building served as a shoemaker's shop in the early 20th century. Over time, the need for the shoemaker's services diminished, and the building was repurposed for various uses.The small size and location of the building made it perfect for an intimate and cozy hotel project.

In 2013, the current owner - Leif Thingtved skælmsk decided to convert the top floor of the building into a hotel room while retaining the ground floor as a cozy café. The idea was to create a unique and exclusive accommodation offering a personalized and memorable experience in the heart of Copenhagen. Since then, the hotel has attracted guests from around the world seeking a different and charming stay.

This one room has become a symbol of the boutique hotel concept, where quality and personalized service are highly valued. It's a small gem in Copenhagen where history, design, and hospitality blend together to create an unforgettable experience.

A COZY CORNER IN THE CITY

In the café at Central Hotel, the decor is part of the experience. Every detail is carefully selected to create an atmosphere of homely comfort and aesthetic appeal. You will notice the soft, cushioned chairs and sofas where you can sink down and relax while enjoying your coffee. The colors and materials have been chosen with care to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where guests immediately feel welcome. The lighting is soft and subdued, and the beautifully arranged plants add a touch of nature and life to the room.

Everywhere you can find small, unique details that give the cafe its own personality and charm, from artwork on the walls to vintage accessories on the shelves. Whether you sit by the window and watch the life of the city or find a cozy corner in the café's deep sofas, you will discover that this small café is more than just a place to have a cup of coffee.

It is a sanctuary from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, where you can relax, find inspiration, and enjoy the simple joys of the moment.
